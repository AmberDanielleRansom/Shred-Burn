Classes Available

We have a variety of classes to choose from weekly!

YOGA

Step into a quiet, peaceful environment and shift your focus. Yoga is a totally different way to strengthen your body through toning, stretching, and lengthening your muscles. You’ll learn to connect your mind, body, and breath to improve flexibility and mind-body balance. Namaste.

HIIT

High Intensity Interval Training is one of the quickest ways to get fit. This never-boring, full-body blend of cardio, strength training, and music is a real calorie crusher. And each class is as unique as the instructor leading it. Come in and burn! This is a fun class to BURN those extra calories!

CYCLE

Beats per minute are the name of the game. Your heart rate, legs, and the music will be pumping as you pedal to next-level fitness in our low-impact, high-intensity cycle classes. The sweat will fly, your legs will burn, and you’re sure to love it. Pedaling nowhere has never been so satisfying!

ZUMBA

Zumba is a fun way to exercise. It’s a dance class that is set to Latin music and it is led by a passionate instructor, who loves to move. Because it’s a fun dance class with simple moves, you’re constantly moving. This means you can actually burn upwards of 600 calories during a single workout class

ABS/CORE

Fire up your midsection to get strong abs! These low-impact core classes can also strengthen your lower back and improve your balance. A strong core will help you avoid injury, making this class the perfect addition to any workout. Get your abs super sculpted and have the tummy you desire

AQUA

Everyone come to the pool for a weightless, total body jam sesh! Feel the beat, break a sweat by following your instructor, and use the water’s natural resistance for the ultimate low-impact workout to tone and build strength and stamina. A perfect class for recovering from injury.
